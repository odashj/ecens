About Us

R&D in Electricity, Environment and Energy

The elements of renewable energy—water, wind, sun, and geothermal—hold the key to the future stability of our world. ECENS pursues the nexus of renewable energy and the energy mix between grid storage, electrified transportation and electricity production. We strive to facilitate this energy handshake.

What we do

ECENS focuses on strategically pursuing game changing technology and science that will bring a paradigm in the green energy economy and our new smart electronic landscape in technology that brings people together for the human experience in a safe and socially responsible way.
